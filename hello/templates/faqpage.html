    Are the product images online an accurate representation of the shoes? +

    While e-commerce platforms strive to provide accurate product images, there may be slight variations in color or appearance due to factors such as screen settings or lighting. It's recommended to read the product descriptions and reviews to gather more information about the shoes before making a purchase.

    If my order has not been shipped yet, can I change my delivery address or cancel/change my order? +

    Your order will begin going through our ordering process as soon as it is placed. Therefore, even if your order has not yet been shipped, we are sadly unable to cancel or change it. You can return any items you don't want after receiving your order by adhering to our return policy. Please get in touch with our Happy Feet shop's customer service department if any order details are incorrect.

    What should I do if I cannot find the product(s) I am searching for? +

    If you are unable to find the product(s) you are searching for, please contact our Happy Feet shop's customer service department. We will do our best to help you find the product(s) you are looking for.
